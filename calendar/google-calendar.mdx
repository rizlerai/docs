## 🗓️ Google Calendar Integration Overview

Seamlessly manage your schedule—view, create, and edit events—from within your Ai sync dashboard with the Google Calendar integration.

## 📅 Calendar Dashboard

![calendar](/images/calendar/image01.png)

### Key Dashboard Features
- **Connected Account**: Displays your active Google account
- **Navigation Controls**: Use arrow buttons (`<`, `>`) to switch months or click "Today" to jump to the current date
- **View Modes**: Toggle between Month, Week, or Day views
- **Event Creation**: Click the "Create Event" button to schedule new events

## 🤖 AI Join Functionality

This integration offers an AI Join feature, enabling AI agents to join meetings for you:

- Displays "AI Join: No Permission (Locked)" on calendars without access
- Allows AI agents to join meetings automatically when enabled
- Handles multiple subscribed calendars (e.g., regional holidays or secondary calendars)

> **Premium Feature**: AI Join requires a premium subscription. Activate the feature by subscribing to allow AI assistants to automatically participate in your scheduled meetings.

![calendar](/images/calendar/image04.png)

## ✍️ Scheduling New Events

Easily create events by clicking "Create Event." The scheduling modal includes:

![calendar](/images/calendar/image02.png)

**Required Event Details:**
- **Title**: Name your event
- **Description**: Add notes or context (resizable input)
- **Start Date & Time**: Choose the event start (format: dd/mm/yyyy, --:-- --)
- **End Date & Time**: Choose when it ends (format: dd/mm/yyyy, --:-- --)

![calendar](/images/calendar/image03.png)

## 📂 Managing Multiple Calendars

- Quickly switch calendars using the dropdown selector
- View all subscribed calendars alongside your main calendar
- Adjust permissions and AI Join settings per individual calendar
