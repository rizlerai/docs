## Account Setup Process

### Step 1: Start Onboarding

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Stripe Setup'">
    From your Rizler dashboard, click the "Stripe Setup" button to begin
  </Step>
  <Step title="Redirect to Stripe">
    You'll be securely redirected to Stripe's onboarding page
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create or Connect Account">
    Either create a new Stripe account or connect your existing one
  </Step>
</Steps>

![Stripe Hosted Onboarding](/images/business-management/stripe-hosted-onboarding.png)

### Step 2: Complete Required Information

You'll need to provide the following information during onboarding:

![Stripe Setup Form](/images/business-management/stripe-setup-form.png)

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Business Information">
    - **Business Name**: Your agency or company name
    - **Business Type**: LLC, Corporation, Sole Proprietorship, etc.
    - **Business Address**: Your registered business address
    - **Tax ID**: EIN or SSN for tax reporting
    - **Business Description**: What services you provide
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Bank Account Details">
    - **Bank Name**: Your business bank name
    - **Account Number**: Bank account for receiving payments
    - **Routing Number**: Bank routing number
    - **Account Type**: Checking or Savings
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Personal Information">
    - **Full Name**: Legal name of business owner
    - **Date of Birth**: For identity verification
    - **Address**: Personal address of business owner
    - **Phone Number**: Contact number
    - **Government ID**: Driver's license or passport
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Additional Documents">
    - **Business License**: If required in your industry
    - **Articles of Incorporation**: For corporations/LLCs
    - **Bank Statements**: Recent business bank statements
    - **Tax Documents**: Recent tax returns if requested
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 3: Account Verification

After submitting your information:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Automatic Verification">
    Stripe automatically verifies most information within minutes
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manual Review (if needed)">
    Some accounts may require additional review (1-7 business days)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Notification">
    You'll receive email updates about your verification status
  </Step>
  <Step title="Account Activation">
    Once approved, your account is ready to receive payments
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Questions

### Account Setup Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why was my account rejected?">
    **Common reasons:**
    - Incomplete business information
    - Industry restrictions (high-risk businesses)
    - Suspicious or fraudulent activity
    - Previous violations of Stripe's terms
    
    **What to do:**
    - Contact Stripe support directly
    - Provide additional documentation
    - Clarify your business model
    - Wait for manual review if required
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does verification take?">
    **Typical timelines:**
    - **Instant**: Most accounts (90%+)
    - **1-2 days**: Accounts needing additional review
    - **3-7 days**: Complex businesses or missing documents
    - **Longer**: High-risk industries or compliance issues
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I need to update my information?">
    **You can update:**
    - Bank account information
    - Business address
    - Contact information
    - Tax details
    
    **How to update:**
    - Log into your Stripe dashboard
    - Navigate to Account Settings
    - Make changes and submit for review
    - Some changes may require re-verification
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Payment and Payout Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why haven't I received my payout?">
    **Common reasons:**
    - Bank account information is incorrect
    - Your account has a hold or restriction
    - Weekend or bank holiday delays
    - First payout takes longer (7-14 days)
    
    **Check these:**
    - Verify bank account details in Stripe dashboard
    - Look for any account notifications or restrictions
    - Contact your bank about incoming ACH transfers
    - Check Stripe's payout schedule
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change my payout schedule?">
    **Available options:**
    - **Daily**: Fastest option (default)
    - **Weekly**: Once per week
    - **Monthly**: Once per month
    - **Manual**: On-demand payouts
    
    **How to change:**
    - Log into your Stripe dashboard
    - Go to Settings → Payouts
    - Select your preferred schedule
    - Changes take effect immediately
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What fees does Stripe charge?">
    **Standard processing fees:**
    - **Credit Cards**: 2.9% + 30¢ per transaction
    - **ACH/Bank Transfers**: 0.8% (capped at $5)
    - **Currency Conversion**: 1% fee
    
    **Additional fees:**
    - Chargeback fee: $15 per dispute (handled by agency)
    - Failed payment fee: None
    - Payout fees: None for standard schedule
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
