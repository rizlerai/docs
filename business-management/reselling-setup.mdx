The **Reselling Module** is a comprehensive package management system for creating, configuring, and managing reselling packages for your clients. This module offers flexible pricing (including slider-based multipliers and manual entry), seamless Stripe payment integration, and detailed profit calculations.

---

### Key Features
- ✅ **Dual Pricing Modes**: Use sliders for multiplier-based pricing or enter values manually for precise control.
- ✅ **Dynamic Profit Calculations**: Instantly see fixed and percentage-based Rizler charges and their impact on profits.
- ✅ **Stripe Integration**: Secure, built-in payment processing to enable customer transactions.
- ✅ **Module Management**: Select and assign specific features and services to each package.
- ✅ **Real-time Updates**: Live calculation and profit margin updates as you configure your packages.



## 📦 Package Management



**Accessing the Module**

1. Navigate to **Setup** → **Reselling** in the main menu.
2. You'll see the Reselling dashboard with your existing service packages displayed in a comprehensive overview table:
   ![Reselling Package Overview](/images/business-management/overview.png)
3. Click **"Add"** to create a new package, or **"Edit"** to modify an existing package.


![Add New Package Interface](/images/business-management/add-new1.png)

Ready to launch a new offer that wows your clients? Just follow these simple steps and watch your service lineup shine:

1. **Click** the vibrant **"Add"** button on the Reselling dashboard to start something new.
2. **Give your package a standout name**—think catchy, clear, and appealing, such as _"Premium Advantage"_ or _"Pro Success Kit"_.
3. **Save your title** with a click on **"Save Title"**, making it official.
4. **Set enticing prices** for all services—choose slider mode for dynamic rates or manual input for custom deals.
5. **Pick powerful modules and features** to pack your offering with value.
6. **Seal the deal** by hitting **"Save"**. Your new package is ready to impress!

# 🎚️ Flexible Pricing

🌈 **Choose your perfect pricing style—maximum control, minimum hassle:**

- 🟰 **Effortless Markup**: Slide between **1x**–**100x** to set your ideal profit multiplier instantly.
- 👁️‍🗨️ **Live Price Preview**: See the customer price adjust in real time as you move the slider—no guesswork!

- 🛡️ **Margin Guard**: The platform won't accept prices below your real cost, so every sale is protected.
- ✅ **Quick Save**: Just tap the 💾 save icon—your edits lock in instantly.
- 📈 **Instant Profit**: See your projected profit and margin live as you dial in each value.

## 🚀 Advanced Features


- **Fixed Charge**:  
  Set a simple, predictable dollar value (e.g., **"$2.00/mo"**) for each package.  
  > 🧮 **Your Profit** = Customer Charge − Fixed Charge  
- **Percentage Charge**:  
  Use a flexible rate (e.g., **"15%"**) to automatically scale your fees as revenue grows.  
  > 🧮 **Your Profit** = Customer Charge − (Charge × Percentage)

🌟 **Handy Examples:**  
- Sell for **$100** with a **$20** fixed charge → **You keep $80**  
- Sell for **$100** with a **15%** charge → **You keep $85**
