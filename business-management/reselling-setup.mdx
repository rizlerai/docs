Configure and manage your reseller program, partner relationships, and pricing packages within the Rizler platform.

## üìã Overview

The Reselling Setup module allows you to manage your complete reseller operation including Stripe payment integration, contact information, payout details, and comprehensive package management for all your services.

## üöÄ Initial Setup

### üí≥ Stripe Payment Integration

To begin accepting payments for your reseller program, you need to configure Stripe integration:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Stripe Setup">
    Click the "Stripe Setup" button to configure your payment processing
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Account">
    Connect your Stripe account to enable payment processing
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Webhooks">
    Set up webhooks for real-time payment notifications
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test Integration">
    Verify payment processing with test transactions
  </Step>
</Steps>

## üí∞ Payout Information

### üè¶ Payment Configuration

Set up your payout preferences and banking information:

- **Payment Methods**: Configure preferred payment methods for commissions
- **Banking Details**: Add bank account information for direct deposits
- **Payment Schedule**: Set up automatic payout frequency (daily, weekly, monthly)
- **Minimum Payout**: Configure minimum payout thresholds
