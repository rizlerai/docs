# Companies

Manage company accounts, organizational settings, and business configurations within the Rizler platform.

## Overview

The Companies section allows you to manage multiple business entities, configure company-specific settings, and organize your business operations efficiently within Rizler.

## Company Management

### Creating a New Company

Add new companies to your Rizler account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Company Information">
    Enter basic company details including name, industry, and contact information
  </Step>
  <Step title="Business Settings">
    Configure company-specific settings and preferences
  </Step>
  <Step title="User Assignment">
    Assign users and define access permissions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Integration Setup">
    Configure integrations and external connections
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Company Configuration

### General Settings

Configure core company settings:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Company Profile">
    - **Company Name**: Legal business name and display name
    - **Industry**: Business industry classification
    - **Size**: Company size and employee count
    - **Timezone**: Primary business timezone
    - **Currency**: Default currency for billing and reporting
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Contact Information">
    - **Business Address**: Primary business location
    - **Phone Numbers**: Main business phone and support lines
    - **Email Addresses**: Primary contact and support emails
    - **Website**: Company website and social media links
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Branding">
    - **Company Logo**: Upload and manage company logos
    - **Brand Colors**: Define brand color schemes
    - **Custom Themes**: Configure platform appearance
    - **White Labeling**: Custom branding options for enterprise
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
