## üö¶ Column Visibility Controls

- Use the **Show/Hide Columns** dropdown (at top of table) to toggle any column.
- Uncheck a field to immediately hide it from the grid; re-check to make it visible.
- Column visibility settings are remembered per session for user convenience.
- If no columns are selected, a warning prompts you to select at least one field.
