# Contacts

Comprehensive contact management system for organizing, tracking, and managing customer relationships and interactions across all your campaigns and business activities.

## Overview

The Contacts system provides a centralized database for managing all customer and prospect information, enabling efficient relationship management, detailed tracking, and personalized customer interactions.

## Contact Management

### Contact Creation
- **Manual Entry**: Add contacts individually with complete information
- **Bulk Import**: Import contacts from CSV, Excel, or other data sources
- **Form Integration**: Capture contacts from web forms and landing pages
- **API Integration**: Add contacts programmatically through APIs

### Contact Information
- **Basic Details**: Name, email, phone, and address information
- **Custom Fields**: Business-specific and custom data fields
- **Contact Preferences**: Communication preferences and settings
- **Relationship Data**: Company affiliations and relationships

### Contact Organization
- **Contact Grouping**: Organize contacts by categories or segments
- **Folder Structure**: Hierarchical organization of contact groups
- **Search & Filter**: Advanced search and filtering capabilities
- **Duplicate Management**: Identify and merge duplicate contacts
