# SMS Template

Create, manage, and customize SMS templates for automated messaging campaigns, customer communications, and marketing outreach.

## Overview

SMS templates provide pre-designed message formats for consistent, professional customer communication. Streamline your messaging workflows with customizable templates for various use cases.

## Template Categories

### Marketing Templates
- **Promotional Messages**: Special offers and discounts
- **Product Announcements**: New product launches
- **Event Invitations**: Event notifications and invites
- **Seasonal Campaigns**: Holiday and seasonal messaging

### Operational Templates
- **Appointment Reminders**: Automated appointment notifications
- **Payment Reminders**: Billing and payment notifications
- **Delivery Updates**: Shipping and delivery status
- **Service Notifications**: System and service updates

### Customer Service Templates
- **Welcome Messages**: New customer onboarding
- **Thank You Messages**: Post-purchase appreciation
- **Feedback Requests**: Customer satisfaction surveys
- **Support Responses**: Common support scenarios

## Dynamic Variables

### Contact Variables
- **{{first_name}}**: Customer first name
- **{{last_name}}**: Customer last name
- **{{phone_number}}**: Customer phone number
- **{{email}}**: Customer email address
- **{{company}}**: Customer company name

### Business Variables
- **{{business_name}}**: Your business name
- **{{business_phone}}**: Your business phone
- **{{business_address}}**: Your business address
- **{{website}}**: Your website URL

### Date/Time Variables
- **{{current_date}}**: Current date
- **{{current_time}}**: Current time
- **{{appointment_date}}**: Appointment date
- **{{appointment_time}}**: Appointment time
