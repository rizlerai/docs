## üè¢ Basic Information

Update and complete your company details for compliance and platform requirements:

- **Entity Type**: Select the legal structure of your business (e.g., Private, Public, LLC, Nonprofit, Partnership)
- **Company Name**: Enter your full legal company name as registered
- **Rizz Dial Industry**: Choose the industry your business operates in (e.g., Professional, Engineering, Technology)
- **Twilio Industry**: Select your industry classification as required by Twilio
- **EIN**: Enter your 9-digit Employer Identification Number (EIN)
- **EIN Issuing Country**: Select the country that issued your EIN (e.g., US)
- **Website**: Enter your company website (without http:// or https://)
- **Stock Symbol** (Optional): Enter your stock ticker symbol (e.g., AAPL), if applicable
- **Stock Exchange** (Optional): Select the relevant stock exchange (e.g., NASDAQ), if applicable
- **Business Type**: Specify your business type (e.g., Partnership)
- **Business Regions of Operation**: Indicate areas where your business operates (e.g., USA and Canada)
- **Business Registration Identifier**: Enter your business registration ID (e.g., EIN, DUNS, etc.)
- **Alternate Business ID** (Optional): Provide an additional business identifier (e.g., DUNS number)
- **Alt Business ID Type** (Optional): Choose the type for your alternate business ID (e.g., DUNS)
- **Social Media Profile URLs** (Optional): Add social media links (comma separated)
- **Testing**: (Field in use for internal/testing purposes or notes)

## üè† Address Details
Complete business address information:
- **Customer Name**: Name of the customer or business entity at this address
- **Street**: Primary business address (required)
- **Street Secondary** (Optional): Secondary or suite/apartment details
- **City**: Business location city (required)
- **State**: Select from dropdown menu (required)
- **Postal Code**: ZIP/postal code (required)
- **Country**: Select your country (required)

![Business Detail](/images/settings/business-detail/image2.png)

## üë§ Authorized Contact Details
Primary contact information for business verification:
- **First Name**: Contact person's first name (required)
- **Last Name**: Contact person's last name (required)
- **Email**: Official contact email address (required)
- **Title**: Contact person's title (required)  
  *Example: "Master"*
- **Job Title**: Contact person's specific role or position (select from options, required)  
  *Example: "DIRECTOR"*
- **Phone**: Direct contact phone number (required, include country code)
