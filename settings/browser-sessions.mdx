View and manage all active browser sessions across your devices. Each session shows browser type, device info, IP address, and last activity.

## Logout Options

- **Logout Other Devices** - End all sessions except current one
- **Individual Logout** - End specific sessions only  
- **Logout All** - Terminate all sessions including current

Sessions automatically expire after inactivity. You'll receive email alerts for new device logins and suspicious activity.
