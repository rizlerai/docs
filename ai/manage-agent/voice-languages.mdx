#### 🎙️ User Guide

The **Voice & Language** tab is where you choose **how your agent thinks (in text)**, **how it sounds**, **which language it uses**, and **optional background sound on calls**.

Small **information icons** next to labels give short hints when you point at them.

<img
  src="/images/ai/AI-agent/edit/voices-languages/overview.png"
  alt="Voice and Language tab overview"
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm"
/>

## 🗂️ Opening the tab

Select **Voice & Language** in the row of tabs at the top of the agent settings. The screen is laid out in two columns: model and voice on the first row; language and background sound on the second.


## 🤖 AI Model

**What it does**  
This is the “brain” behind your agent’s replies. Different options can differ in quality, speed, and **cost per minute** (shown next to each name).

**How to use it**

## 🎤 Voice

**What it does**  
Sets the **spoken voice** your agent uses on calls—tone and style depend on the voice you pick.

**What you see on the tab**  
A field shows your **current voice** as a name and an **identifier** in parentheses. Next to it is a **settings (gear)** button (see [⚙️ Voice Settings window](#voice-settings-window)).

**How to pick or change a voice**  
The main catalog opens in a large **Voice List** window (scrollable). There you can hear samples, filter, search, and confirm a voice with **Use This**.

**Tooltip**  
Explains that different voices have different tones and speaking styles.

## 📋 Voice List window (voice catalog)

This window helps you browse all voices that are **available for your agent**.

<img
  src="/images/ai/AI-agent/edit/voices-languages/voice-lists.png"
  alt="Voice List catalog with filters and table"
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm"
/>

**Filters (top row)**

- **Gender:** All, Male, or Female  
- **Accent:** All, American, or British  
- **Provider:** All, Eleven Labs, Deepgram, Open AI, or Custom Voice  
- **Search:** Type to narrow the list by text you see in the table  

**Table columns**

- **Play:** Hear a short sample (play / pause behavior).  
- **Voice:** Picture and **display name**.  
- **Trait:** Badges for **accent**, **age**, and **gender** when that information exists.  
- **Voice ID:** The label used to identify that voice in the system.  
- **Action:** **Use This** — selects that voice for your agent and applies it in line with the rest of the page.

**Footer**

- **Close** — exits without applying a new voice via **Use This**.  
- **Save Changes** — appears in the footer; the main way to apply a voice is **Use This** on a row.



## ⚙️ Voice Settings window (gear button next to Voice) {#voice-settings-window}

**What it does**  
Fine-tunes **how the agent’s voice behaves**, separate from *which* voice you chose.

<img
  src="/images/ai/AI-agent/edit/voices-languages/voice-setting.png"
  alt="Voice Speed, Temperature, and Volume sliders"
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm"
/>

**Fields inside**

1. **Voice Speed**  
   A draggable control from slow to fast (ends labeled roughly **0.00** to **2.00**).  
   When you finish adjusting (release the control), the new speed is saved. The number in the middle updates as you move it.

2. **Voice Temperature**  
   A draggable control on the same kind of scale.  
   This affects how **expressive or varied** the speech feels (higher often means more variation).  
   When you release the control, the value is saved.

3. **Voice Volume**  
   A draggable control for how **loud** the agent’s voice is (same scale style).  
   When you release the control, the value is saved.

**Footer**  
**Close** — closes the window. Changes to the three controls are applied when you **release** each control, not from a separate Save button inside this window (there is no active Save button for these three in the footer).

**Defaults**  
If no value was stored before, speed, temperature, and volume each start around **1**.



## 🌐 Language

**What it does**  
Sets the **main language** for the agent. This affects **understanding what callers say** and **how replies are formed**.

**How to use it**  
Open the list and choose one language. When your choice changes, the system tries to save it right away.

**If nothing is selected**  
If the list is left on **Select Language**, the product may warn you to choose a language first and will not treat that as a valid save.

## 🎵 Background Sound

**What it does**  
Adds a **soft ambient sound** behind the call so the call can feel more natural or help mask noise.

<img
  src="/images/ai/AI-agent/edit/voices-languages/background-sound.png"
  alt="Background sound dropdown"
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm"
/>

**Main list on the tab**

- **Select Sound** — means **no** background sound (same as turning it off).  
- **Cafe Ambience**  
- **Event Hall Background**  
- **Open Air - Summer**  
- **Open Air - Mountain**  
- **Clean White Noise**  
- **Office Chatter**  

When you change this list (after the page has finished loading), your choice is saved automatically.

**Gear button (next to Background Sound)**  
Opens **Background Sound Volume** (see [🔊 Background Sound Volume window](#background-sound-volume-window)).

**Tooltip**  
Explains using ambient sound for a more professional feel or to mask background noise.



## 🔊 Background Sound Volume window (gear next to Background Sound) {#background-sound-volume-window}

**What it does**  
Sets how **strong** the background sound is—not the agent’s voice volume (that is under **Voice Settings**).

<img
  src="/images/ai/AI-agent/edit/voices-languages/background-sound-volume.png"
  alt="Background sound volume slider and Save"
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm"
/>

**Control**  
A **Volume** bar from **0** to **2**, with the current level shown in the middle as you move it.

**Save**  
Press **Save** to store the volume. You must have a **real** background sound selected (not “Select Sound” / off); otherwise you may get a message asking you to pick a background sound first.

**Close**  
Closes the window without using **Save** for that visit (your last saved volume remains whatever was saved successfully before).
