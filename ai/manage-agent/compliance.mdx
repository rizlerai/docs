## 🕐 Section 1 — Schedule

<img
  src="/images/ai/AI-agent/edit/compliance/section-1.png"
  alt="Section 1 — Schedule settings in the Dialing Compliance tab"
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm"
/>

**Question it answers:** *When is this agent allowed to dial?*

### ⏰ Earliest & latest dial time

Set a **start time** and **end time** for the calling window.

- Any call scheduled outside this window is **automatically held** and queued until the next allowed time slot — it does not get skipped or lost, just delayed.
- Example: Set `09:00` to `17:00` and the agent will only call between 9 AM and 5 PM.



### 📅 Calling days

Seven day-chips appear: **Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri, Sat, Sun**

- Click a chip to **turn that day on** (highlighted = active).
- Click again to **turn it off**.
- The agent will only dial on days that are active.

| Common setup | Days to activate |
|---|---|
| Standard business week | Mon → Fri |
| Including Saturday outreach | Mon → Sat |
| 7-day campaign | All 7 days |


## 🗺️ Section 2 — Calling region

<img
  src="/images/ai/AI-agent/edit/compliance/section-2.png"
  alt="Section 2 — Calling region settings in the Dialing Compliance tab"
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm"
/>

**Question it answers:** *Which US states is this agent allowed to call into?*

### 🌎 The interactive state map

A **hex-grid map of the United States** lets you visually select states one by one. Each state is a clickable hexagon.

- **Click a state** → it highlights (selected / allowed).
- **Click again** → it deactivates (blocked).

#### 🔗 Quick-action links above the map

| Link | What it does |
|---|---|
| **⊕ Select all 50** | Activates every state at once |
| **⊖ Clear all** | Removes all state selections |
| **📋 View legal hours by state** | Opens a reference table (see below) |

#### ✅ No states selected = all states allowed

If you leave the map completely empty (no states highlighted), the agent treats that as **no restriction** — it can call all 50 states. Select specific states only if you want to **restrict** calling to certain regions.

### 📋 Legal hours reference table

Click **"View legal hours by state"** to open a popup table showing every state's legal calling window, for example:

| State | Weekdays | Sunday | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Texas | 9am – 9pm | 12pm – 9pm | Sundays restricted |
| California | 8am – 9pm | 8am – 9pm | Federal floor |
| Florida | 8am – 8pm | 8am – 8pm | Stricter than federal |

This is a **reference only**. To actually enforce these state-specific rules automatically, you need to turn on the **Honor State Legal Hours** toggle in Section 3 below.


## 🛡️ Section 3 — Compliance layer

<img
  src="/images/ai/AI-agent/edit/compliance/section-3.png"
  alt="Section 3 — Compliance layer settings in the Dialing Compliance tab"
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm"
/>

**Question it answers:** *What extra automatic protections should be active?*

Three toggles, each controlling a different layer of protection.

### ⚖️ Toggle 1 — Honor state legal hours

| | OFF (default) | ON |
|---|---|---|
| **What happens** | Only your schedule (Section 1) is used | The system also checks the **contact's local time** in their state before dialing |

**Example of why this matters:**

Your schedule says 8 AM – 9 PM. A contact lives in Florida where calls must stop at 8 PM. Without this toggle, the agent might call that Florida contact at 8:30 PM your time. With this toggle ON, the system sees it is 8:30 PM in Florida and **holds the call** until the next allowed window.

**Turn this ON if:**

- You are calling contacts across multiple US states
- Your contact list spans different time zones
- You want automatic per-state legal protection without managing it manually

### 🚫 Toggle 2 — Honor DNC scrubbing

**DNC = Do Not Call registry** — a federal list of phone numbers that have opted out of receiving telemarketing calls.

| | OFF | ON |
|---|---|---|
| **What happens** | All numbers are dialed regardless | Numbers on the DNC registry are automatically skipped |



To use real-time DNC scrubbing, you need a DNC provider connected in your **account settings**. Once connected, every number is checked against the registry before the call is placed.

### ⚠️ Toggle 3 — Override the schedule

This is a **high-risk setting** — read carefully before enabling.

When this is turned **ON**:

- The agent ignores **all time restrictions** you set in Section 1
- The agent ignores **all state legal hours** from Section 2
- The agent can dial **24 hours a day, 7 days a week**
- A bright warning banner appears on screen confirming the override is active

**By enabling this toggle, you confirm:**

- You personally accept full legal responsibility for any compliance issues
- You have the legal authorization to call outside standard hours
- The platform is providing this as a tool, not endorsing the practice

**Only use this for:**

- Emergency or time-sensitive services with prior express consent
- Situations where you have confirmed written authorization from contacts
- Internal testing environments (never production calling lists)


**📢 The override warning banner**

When Override is active, a blue information banner appears below the compliance settings:

> **Override active.** The schedule and state legal hours are bypassed for this agent. Logged on the agent record.

This banner is a reminder — it does not go away until you turn the override off.


## 💾 Saving your settings

Click the **Save compliance settings** button at the bottom.

- A success message confirms the save: `✓ Saved`
- Changes take effect on the **next call** placed by the agent
