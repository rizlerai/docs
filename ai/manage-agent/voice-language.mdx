## Background Sound Volume Control

### Volume Settings

Configure background sound volume levels for optimal audio experience:

**Volume Slider**: 
- **Current Setting**: 0.6 
- **Range**: 0 (Silent) to 2 (Maximum)
- **Function**: Adjusts the intensity of background ambient sound

**Control Actions**:
- **Save**: Apply volume changes to your agent configuration
- **Close**: Exit volume settings without saving changes
