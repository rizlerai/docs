## ‚ùì What is a 10DLC Brand?

A 10DLC Brand is your business's verified digital identity within the messaging ecosystem. Think of it as your "business license" for professional SMS messaging. It represents:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Business Identity" icon="building">
    Your legal company information, cross-verified with state business registries and federal databases
  </Card>
  <Card title="Trust Score" icon="star">
    Carrier-verified reputation (0-100 scale) affecting message deliverability, sending volume limits, and filtering bypass
  </Card>
  <Card title="Compliance Profile" icon="shield">
    Regulatory standing ensuring adherence to TCPA, CAN-SPAM, and carrier-specific messaging guidelines
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
**Why Brand Registration Matters:** Unregistered messaging can result in 70%+ delivery failures, automatic spam filtering, and potential number blocking. Registered brands see 95%+ delivery rates and can send up to 100,000+ messages daily.
</Info>

**üßæ Registration Form Fields**

The registration form contains three essential fields that determine your brand's approval and capabilities:

| Field | Description | Requirements | Impact |
|-------|-------------|--------------|---------|
| **Brand Alias*** | Your chosen brand identifier | Required, unique, 2-50 characters | Used for identification and reporting |
| **Profile*** | Associated 10DLC profile | Must select from existing profiles | Determines business verification level |
| **Brand Type** | Brand classification | Auto-populated based on profile | Affects messaging volume and features |

<Info>
**Required Fields:** Fields marked with (*) are mandatory and must be completed before submission. The form includes real-time validation to prevent submission errors.
</Info>

<Warning>
**Profile Dependency:** Brand Type options are dynamically populated based on your selected profile. Ensure you have created a 10DLC profile before registering a brand.
</Warning>
