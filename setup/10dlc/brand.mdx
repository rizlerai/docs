# 10DLC Brand

Register and manage your brand within the 10DLC ecosystem to establish trust and improve message deliverability for your business communications.

## Overview

A 10DLC Brand represents your business identity in the messaging ecosystem. Brand registration is a crucial step that establishes your legitimacy with mobile carriers and helps ensure your messages reach their intended recipients.

## What is a 10DLC Brand?

Your 10DLC Brand includes:

- **Brand Identity**: Your business name, logo, and identifying information
- **Business Credentials**: Verification documents and compliance status
- **Trust Score**: Carrier-assigned score based on your business reputation
- **Use Case Approvals**: Approved messaging scenarios for your brand

## Brand Trust Scores

### Understanding Trust Scores

Carriers assign trust scores to brands based on various factors:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="High Trust" icon="shield-check">
    **Score: 75-100**
    - Established businesses
    - Strong verification
    - Clean compliance history
  </Card>
  <Card title="Medium Trust" icon="shield">
    **Score: 50-74**
    - Verified businesses
    - Standard documentation
    - No major compliance issues
  </Card>
  <Card title="Low Trust" icon="shield-exclamation">
    **Score: 0-49**
    - New businesses
    - Limited verification
    - Requires additional validation
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Factors Affecting Trust Score

- **Business Age**: How long the business has been operating
- **Verification Level**: Completeness of submitted documentation
- **Industry Type**: Some industries have inherent trust implications
- **Compliance History**: Past messaging compliance record
- **Financial Stability**: Business financial standing and credit
- **Digital Footprint**: Online presence and reputation

## Brand Management

### Updating Brand Information

To modify your brand registration:

1. **Access Brand Settings**: Navigate to Setup > 10DLC > Brand
2. **Request Modification**: Submit change request with supporting documentation
3. **Carrier Review**: Wait for carrier approval of changes
4. **Update Confirmation**: Verify changes are reflected in your account

<Warning>
  Brand modifications may affect your trust score and require re-verification of use cases.
</Warning>

### Brand Status Monitoring

Track your brand status through the dashboard:

- **Pending**: Registration submitted, awaiting review
- **Verified**: Brand approved and active
- **Suspended**: Compliance issues require resolution
- **Rejected**: Additional documentation or corrections needed

## Brand Portfolio Management

### Multiple Brands

If you operate multiple business entities:

- **Separate Registrations**: Each legal entity requires separate brand registration
- **Shared Resources**: Some verification documents can be shared
- **Individual Scoring**: Each brand receives its own trust score
- **Distinct Use Cases**: Each brand may have different approved use cases

### Brand Consolidation

Consider consolidating brands when:

- Operating under a single legal entity
- Using consistent messaging practices
- Simplifying compliance management
- Reducing administrative overhead

<Note>
  Consult with legal and compliance teams before making brand consolidation decisions.
</Note>
