# 10DLC Campaign

Create and manage 10DLC campaigns to define your messaging use cases and ensure compliance with carrier requirements for A2P messaging.

## Overview

A 10DLC Campaign defines the specific use case for your messaging operations. Each campaign must be approved by carriers and specifies what types of messages you can send, to whom, and under what circumstances.

## What is a 10DLC Campaign?

A 10DLC Campaign includes:

- **Use Case Definition**: The specific purpose of your messaging
- **Message Content**: Types of messages you plan to send
- **Target Audience**: Who will receive your messages
- **Opt-in Process**: How you obtain consent to message
- **Sample Messages**: Examples of actual message content

## Campaign Approval Process

### Review Timeline

Campaign approval typically takes:

- **Standard Use Cases**: 5-10 business days
- **Special Use Cases**: 10-15 business days
- **High-Risk Industries**: 15-30 business days

### Approval Factors

Carriers evaluate campaigns based on:

- **Brand Trust Score**: Higher scores get faster approval
- **Use Case Legitimacy**: Clear business purpose
- **Compliance History**: Past messaging behavior
- **Content Quality**: Professional, compliant message samples
- **Opt-in Process**: Clear and documented consent procedures

### Monitoring Campaign Performance

Track key metrics for your campaigns:

- **Opt-out Rates**: Rate of unsubscribe requests

### Campaign Modifications

To modify an existing campaign:

1. **Submit Change Request**: Describe proposed modifications
2. **Provide Documentation**: Support changes with evidence
3. **Carrier Review**: Wait for approval of modifications
4. **Implementation**: Apply approved changes

<Note>
  Significant campaign changes may require full re-approval and could affect throughput.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting

### Common Campaign Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Campaign Rejection">
    **Causes**: Unclear use case, poor sample messages, inadequate opt-in process
    **Solutions**: Clarify purpose, improve message quality, strengthen consent procedures
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Low Throughput Assignment">
    **Causes**: Low brand trust score, high-risk use case, new business
    **Solutions**: Improve brand verification, demonstrate compliance history, start small and scale
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Delivery Issues">
    **Causes**: Compliance violations, carrier filtering, volume spikes
    **Solutions**: Review message content, ensure opt-in compliance, monitor sending patterns
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
