# 10DLC Profile

Set up and manage your 10DLC profile to ensure compliance with A2P (Application-to-Person) messaging regulations and improve message deliverability.

## Overview

10DLC (10-Digit Long Code) is a system that allows businesses to send A2P SMS messages through standard 10-digit phone numbers while maintaining high deliverability and compliance with carrier requirements.

## What is a 10DLC Profile?

Your 10DLC profile serves as your business identity within the 10DLC ecosystem and includes:

- **Business Information**: Company details, industry, and contact information
- **Messaging Use Cases**: Types of messages you plan to send
- **Compliance Status**: Verification and approval status
- **Throughput Limits**: Approved messaging volume and rate limits

### Verification Process

1. **Document Review**: Carriers review submitted documentation
2. **Business Validation**: Verification of business legitimacy
3. **Website Review**: Assessment of business website and policies
4. **Approval Decision**: Final approval or request for additional information

<Warning>
  Verification can take 7-15 business days. Ensure all documents are current and accurate to avoid delays.
</Warning>

## Messaging Volume and Throughput

### Volume Tiers

10DLC profiles are assigned volume tiers based on business verification:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Starter" icon="seedling">
    **Up to 3,000 messages/day**
    - Basic verification
    - Standard throughput
    - Most common for small businesses
  </Card>
  <Card title="Standard" icon="chart-line">
    **Up to 100,000 messages/day**
    - Enhanced verification
    - Higher throughput
    - Suitable for growing businesses
  </Card>
  <Card title="High Volume" icon="rocket">
    **1M+ messages/day**
    - Full verification required
    - Premium throughput
    - Enterprise-level messaging
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Throughput Rates

- **Base Throughput**: 1 message per second per number
- **Enhanced Throughput**: Up to 10 messages per second (verification required)
- **Carrier-Specific**: Different rates for different carriers

## Profile Management

### Updating Profile Information

To update your 10DLC profile:

1. **Navigate to Setup > 10DLC > Profile**
2. **Click "Edit Profile"**
3. **Update required information**
4. **Submit for review**

<Note>
  Profile changes may require re-verification and could temporarily affect messaging capabilities.
</Note>

### Monitoring Status

Track your profile status through the dashboard:

- **Pending**: Initial submission under review
- **Approved**: Profile verified and active
- **Suspended**: Compliance issues detected
- **Rejected**: Additional information required
