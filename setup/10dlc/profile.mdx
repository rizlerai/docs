Create and manage your business profiles for compliant Application-to-Person (A2P) messaging across both Plivo and Twilio providers. Your 10DLC profile serves as the foundation for all messaging operations, ensuring regulatory compliance and optimal deliverability.


### üìä Dashboard Overview

The 10DLC Profile dashboard gives you a clear, tabular view of all your business profiles and their compliance status. Below is a representative screenshot of the dashboard interface:


 **Profile Table Columns:**
- **Profile Alias**: Internal friendly name for your profile (e.g., "Company MainProfile", "Demo Test")
- **Company Name**: Your business's legal name (must match registration)
- **Customer Type**: Select between "DIRECT" (registered business) or "RESELLER"
- **Entity Type**: Legal structure, such as "PRIVATE", "GOVERNMENT", etc.
- **Industry**: Select the industry classification for your business (e.g., "PROFESSIONAL")
- **Rizz CMS Status**: Shows CMS (Campaign Management System) integration or link status for Rizz (toggle/indicator)
- **Created At**: Date and time the profile was created
- **Action**: Options to edit or delete a profile (with confirmation)

 **Twilio Status Indicators:**
- üü¢ **Active**/**Approved**: Profile active and messaging enabled
- üü° **Pending**: Awaiting review by carrier
- üü† **In Review**: Profile is under carrier examination
- üî¥ **Twilio Rejected**: Carrier denied the profile‚Äîreview the provided feedback before resubmitting
- ‚ö´ **Disabled**: Profile deactivated; messaging blocked

_Note: Use the "Add Profile" button to create a new 10DLC profile. Use the red "Cancel" button in Actions to remove the draft profile before completion._

For complete transparency, the dashboard also lists the total number of profiles and supports sorting by any field.

| Field | Description | Validation Rules | Example |

| **State*** | Business state | Valid US state | "California" |
| **Postal Code*** | ZIP code | 5 or 9 digit format | "94105" or "94105-1234" |
| **Country*** | Business country | Default: "US" | "United States" |

**Edit Capabilities:**
- **Basic Information**: Update company details and contact info
- **Business Details**: Modify industry classifications and website
- **Address Updates**: Change business address and contact details
- **Additional Information**: Update volume estimates and identifiers

**Edit Restrictions:**
- **EIN Changes**: Requires new profile creation
- **Company Name**: Major changes need re-verification
- **Entity Type**: Cannot be changed after approval
- **Provider Settings**: Read-only after initial setup
